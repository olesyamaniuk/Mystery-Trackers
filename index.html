Game Description

Mystery Trackers 20: Detective takes you deep into a hidden object adventure where nothing is as it seems!
As the last surviving agent, you must investigate the destruction of the Mystery Trackers headquarters and track down the forces behind it.
Explore forgotten ruins, search for hidden objects, and decipher cryptic puzzles to uncover the dark truth lurking beneath Silent Hollow.
Are you ready to step into the shadows and solve the case?
